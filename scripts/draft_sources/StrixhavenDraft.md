# Strixhaven Draft Guide

## TIPS

1. **Wait to play Prepare Cards** to get max value
2. **Avoid chasing the Converge mechanic**
3. **Play at least 6 removal** and Splash if necessary
4. **No removal?** Go agro + CT (Counterspell)
