## SOS Land Breakdown (Commons/Uncommons Only)

**Basic Lands:** Forest, Island, Mountain, Plains, Swamp

**Special Lands:**
- Terramorphic Expanse (common) - Fetch land
- Skycoach Waypoint (uncommon) - Enters tapped, taps for any color
- Fields of Strife (common) - Taps for R or W
- Forum of Amity (common) - Taps for B or W  
- Paradox Gardens (common) - Taps for G or U
- Spectacle Summit (common) - Taps for R or U
- Titan's Grave (common) - Taps for B or G
