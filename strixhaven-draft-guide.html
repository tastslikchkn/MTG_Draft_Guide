📚 Draft Tips

⏳ Prepare Cards Wait to play Prepare cards to get maximum value from the mechanic
❌ Avoid Converge Don't chase the Converge mechanic — it's not worth prioritizing
🗡️ Removal Count Play at least 6 removal spells. Splash colors if necessary to hit this number
⚡ No Removal? If you lack removal, pivot to aggressive strategies + countermagic
